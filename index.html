Who I Am

I studied film at CU Boulder. Why film? It's because film is my passion. It's even more than that, it's my second nature. In film I value Disegno Interno aspect the most. Disegno Interno is a notion that first appeared among late Renaissance painters, specifically among Mannerism painters. They would use that term to refer to masterpieces that were meaningful and had values and ideals perceived through the image. This notion is easily applied to film. Classic movies such as those of Fellini, Jean-Luc Godard, Kieslowski, Tarkovsky and the movies of late soviet era, they all fall into the category of Disegno Interno movies. This is why I do film.
